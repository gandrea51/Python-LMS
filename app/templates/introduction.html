How to reach us

With the own Car
  • Jump in your car, any car will do—heck, borrow one if you must.
  • Start driving, aimlessly if necessary, but make sure you're heading in the general direction of "East." Or maybe it's "West." Just pick one.
  • Look for signs. If you see a sign with a book icon, you're either near the library or a really literate gas station.
  • When you hit the big roundabout with the weird statue of what might be a horse (or a dragon?), take the third exit—or the second if you're feeling adventurous.
  • Keep going until you see a building that screams "Books inside!" If you see a McDonald's, you're probably hungry, so turn around.
With the Bus
  • Find a bus stop. Any bus stop will do. Just stand there looking confident.
  • Wait for Bus #42—or was it #24?—that heads toward "Library District" (or somewhere that sounds intellectual).
  • Get on and pay the driver with a smile, a ticket, or maybe a story about your love for libraries.
  • Stay on the bus until you see a building with way too many windows and maybe a big "X" on it.
  • If you miss your stop, don’t panic—libraries have books about how to walk back.
