Who we are

A team dedicated to shared knowledge and innovation.

Founders

Alexander Bennett & Olivia Bennett

Alexander and Olivia Bennett, the visionary couple behind the creation of 100Book, share a passion for literature and innovation. Alexander, an avid historian with a knack for strategy, laid the foundation for the bookstore's operations and growth. Olivia, a former editor and literary enthusiast, shaped the creative and community-focused aspects of the business. Together, they dreamed of a space that blends traditional bookselling with modern technology, creating a hub for readers, learners, and creators.

Manager

Sofia Garcia

Sofia Garcia, the dynamic and resourceful manager of 100Book, ensures the bookstore operates smoothly. With years of experience in retail and customer service, Sofia excels at team leadership and problem-solving. Her warm personality and keen business acumen make her approachable to customers and a source of inspiration for staff. Sofia believes every visitor deserves an exceptional experience, blending efficiency with a personal touch.

Content Creator

Mia Anderson

Mia Anderson, the creative force behind 100Book's online presence, is a talented content creator who transforms the bookstore’s vision into engaging visuals and stories. Whether crafting blog posts, curating social media campaigns, or organizing virtual book clubs, Mia’s innovative ideas keep the brand fresh and relatable. A literature graduate with a flair for design, Mia bridges the gap between books and digital storytelling.

Librarians

Samuel Wright & Emily Patel

Samuel Wright and Emily Patel are the knowledgeable and friendly librarians of 100Book, dedicated to connecting readers with the perfect stories. Samuel, a former literature professor, brings depth and expertise to curating the store’s unique collections. Emily, with her background in youth education, excels at organizing events like storytime for kids and literary discussions for young adults. Together, they create a welcoming atmosphere where every reader feels at home.
